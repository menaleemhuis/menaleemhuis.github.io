About Me

I am a researcher at the KRDB research centre for Knowledge-based Artificial Intelligence at the Free University of Bozen-Bolzano, Italy. My research interest lies in the field of neuro-symbolic AI, particularly on the connection between ontologies and embedding techniques.

Research Interests

  • Neuro-Symbolic AI
  • Knowledge Representation
  • Knowledge Base Embeddings
  • Computational Creativity
  • Analogial Proportions
